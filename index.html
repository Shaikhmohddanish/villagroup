DISCOVER UNMATCHED CONNECTIVITY AT DRONAGIRI

Dronagiri is rapidly emerging as Navi Mumbai’s most strategically located and well-connected node — surrounded by creeks and the sea on three sides, and linked seamlessly by road, rail, sea, and soon air. With ongoing infrastructural projects like the Mumbai Trans Harbour Link (MTHL), Navi Mumbai International Airport, and operational railway networks, Dronagiri offers unparalleled access to Mumbai, Panvel, and beyond. Backed by CIDCO’s planned development and proximity to SEZ, this region is set to become a major residential and industrial hub, making it one of the most promising investment destinations in the MMR region.
